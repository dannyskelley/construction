Your single point of contact for any St. Louis build

Hiring a general contractor should make your project easier, not add confusion. At Abstract Construction LLC, you get one experienced project lead who is accountable from the first walk-through through final inspection. We coordinate trades, pull permits, manage schedules, and keep you informed every step of the way.

What we build as your GC

  • Commercial: offices, retail, restaurants, healthcare, and tenant build-outs.
  • Industrial: warehouses, light manufacturing, mezzanines, and structural steel.
  • Residential: additions, kitchen and bath remodels, full renovations.
  • Specialty work: drywall repair, framing, finish carpentry, welding and steel.

What you get with Abstract Construction

  • Licensed, insured, and bondable in MO and IL.
  • Detailed, line-item bids with no vague lump sums.
  • In-house framing, drywall, welding, and finish carpentry crews.
  • Vetted local subcontractors for electrical, plumbing, and HVAC.
  • 10+ years of project history in the St. Louis metro and Metro East.

Frequently asked questions

What does a general contractor in St. Louis do?

A general contractor manages your entire construction project by pulling permits, hiring and scheduling subcontractors, ordering materials, coordinating inspections, and delivering a finished space on time and on budget.

How do I get a quote from a St. Louis general contractor?

Call (618) 771-0767 or use our quote form. We schedule a free on-site walk-through within 1–3 business days and follow up with a detailed line-item estimate.

Do you self-perform or sub everything out?

We self-perform framing, drywall, finish carpentry, welding, and steel fabrication. Trades like electrical, plumbing, and HVAC are handled by our long-term, vetted subcontractors.

Are you bondable?

Yes. Abstract Construction LLC is fully licensed, insured, and bondable for projects in Missouri and Illinois.
