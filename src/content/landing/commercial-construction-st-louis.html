Frequently asked questions

How much does commercial construction cost in St. Louis?

Costs vary widely by scope, finishes, and site conditions. Most St. Louis commercial fit-outs run $80–$250 per square foot, while ground-up builds typically range from $200–$500 per square foot. We provide line-item estimates for free after a brief on-site walk-through.

Are you licensed and insured to build commercially in Missouri and Illinois?

Yes. Abstract Construction LLC is fully licensed and carries general liability and workers' compensation insurance for projects in both Missouri and Illinois. We can furnish a Certificate of Insurance on request.

How long does a typical commercial build take?

Tenant improvements usually take 4–10 weeks from permit approval. Ground-up commercial builds run 6–12 months depending on size, weather, and inspection cadence. We provide a detailed schedule before mobilizing.

Do you handle permits and inspections?

Yes. We pull all required St. Louis City, County, and Metro East permits and coordinate every inspection so you don't have to.
