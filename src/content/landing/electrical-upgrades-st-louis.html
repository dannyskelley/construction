Electrical and plumbing upgrades for St. Louis properties

Outdated lighting, worn fixtures, and leaking plumbing cut into your property value and your tenants patience. Abstract Construction LLC handles electrical upgrades and light plumbing as part of our full-service construction offering, so you get reliable work from a contractor you already trust.

Electrical services

  • Lighting upgrades: LED conversions, recessed lighting, track lighting, and commercial fixture replacements.
  • Outlet and switch upgrades: GFCI outlets, USB outlets, dimmer switches, and panel-ready additions.
  • Rough-in electrical: new circuits for remodels, additions, and tenant build-outs.
  • Smart office technology: networked lighting controls, occupancy sensors, and AV rough-in.
  • Exterior lighting: security lights, parking lot fixtures, and building-mounted illumination.

Plumbing services

  • Fixture replacement: faucets, toilets, sinks, and water heater connections.
  • Leak repair: supply line and drain repairs for commercial and residential spaces.
  • Rough-in plumbing: new bathroom and kitchen rough-ins for remodels and additions.
  • ADA-compliant upgrades: code-compliant fixture heights and accessible bathroom configurations.
