On-site welding for commercial and industrial St. Louis clients

Abstract Construction LLC brings a fully equipped mobile welding crew to your job site. Whether you need structural repairs on an active facility, custom railings for a new build, or equipment reinforcement that can not wait, we show up ready to work.

Mobile welding services we offer

  • Structural welding: beams, columns, connections, and steel repairs for commercial and industrial buildings.
  • Railing and staircase fabrication: custom steel railings, handrails, and stair stringers built and installed on-site.
  • Mezzanine construction: structural mezzanines for warehouses and industrial facilities.
  • Equipment repair and reinforcement: cracked frames, worn fixtures, and structural metal repairs.
  • Custom metalwork: brackets, supports, frames, and one-off fabrications designed to spec.
  • Gate and fence welding: commercial security gates, perimeter fencing, and access control frames.

Why clients choose our welding crew

  • We come to your site fully equipped, no delays for shop turnaround.
  • Experienced welders with commercial and industrial backgrounds.
  • Fully insured for work at active facilities and construction sites.
  • Honest assessments and upfront pricing before work begins.
