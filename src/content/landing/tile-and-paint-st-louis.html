Tile and paint work done right the first time

Abstract Construction LLC brings the same attention to detail to tile and paint work as to major construction projects. Every surface is prepped properly, every line is straight, and every finish is consistent before we call the job done.

Tile services

  • Floor tile installation: ceramic, porcelain, stone, and large-format tiles for commercial and residential floors.
  • Wall and backsplash tile: kitchens, bathrooms, and accent walls installed with precision.
  • Shower and bath surrounds: waterproofed, properly set, and grouted for lasting results.
  • Commercial entry and lobby tile: high-traffic rated tile installed level and on pattern.
  • Tile repair and replacement: cracked, loose, or outdated tile removed and replaced to match.

Painting services

  • Interior commercial painting: offices, suites, common areas, and tenant spaces.
  • Interior residential painting: full home repaints and room-by-room refreshes.
  • Exterior painting: weather-resistant coatings for commercial and residential buildings.
  • Protective and specialty coatings: epoxy floors, anti-graffiti coatings, and industrial-grade finishes.
  • Accent walls and color consulting: help selecting colors that work for your space and brand.

Frequently asked questions

Do you do both tile and painting in St. Louis?

Yes. Abstract Construction LLC self-performs tile installation and painting for commercial and residential clients across the St. Louis metro and Metro East Illinois.

What types of tile work do you install?

We install floor tile, wall tile, backsplashes, shower surrounds, and decorative accent tile for kitchens, bathrooms, commercial entries, and common areas.

Do you match existing paint colors or textures?

Yes. We match existing colors and sheen levels and texture-match walls when patching or repainting portions of a space.
