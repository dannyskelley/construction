The look of wood. The durability of concrete.

Natural wood staircases require constant maintenance, are vulnerable to moisture, and can splinter or warp over time. Concrete staircase wood graining gives you the warmth and visual appeal of hardwood with a surface that outlasts it at a fraction of the upkeep cost.

What we deliver

  • Realistic wood grain textures: custom grain patterns, plank widths, and knot detailing applied directly to concrete.
  • Custom staining and coloring: match existing wood tones or introduce a completely new look for your space.
  • UV and moisture sealing: protective topcoats rated for interior and exterior exposure.
  • Slip-resistant finishes: commercial-grade anti-slip additives available for high-traffic staircases.
  • Repair and refinishing: restore worn or damaged decorative concrete finishes back to like-new condition.

Ideal applications

  • Commercial and office building stairwells and lobbies.
  • Apartment building common staircases.
  • Residential front stoops, back porches, and interior stairs.
  • Restaurant and retail entryways requiring a distinctive finish.

Frequently asked questions

What is concrete wood graining?

Concrete wood graining is a decorative finishing technique that applies color, texture, and grain patterns to concrete surfaces to mimic the appearance of natural wood. The result is highly realistic, far more durable than wood, and requires minimal maintenance.

How long does a concrete staircase wood grain finish last?

A properly applied wood grain finish on concrete will last many years with normal use. The finish is sealed for UV, moisture, and abrasion resistance, making it suitable for interior and exterior staircases.

Is concrete wood graining good for commercial staircases?

Yes. Concrete wood graining is an excellent choice for commercial staircases because it delivers the warmth of wood at a fraction of the maintenance cost, and it can be finished to meet commercial slip-resistance requirements.
