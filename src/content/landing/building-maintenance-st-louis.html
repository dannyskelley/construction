Reliable commercial building maintenance across St. Louis

Property owners and managers rely on Abstract Construction LLC to keep their buildings running. We are a one-call resource for the repairs and upkeep that keep tenants happy, pass inspections, and protect your investment.

Maintenance services we provide

  • Drywall repair and patching: holes, cracks, water damage, and texture matching.
  • Interior and exterior painting: suite refreshes, common area repaints, and touch-ups.
  • Carpentry and millwork: door repairs, trim replacement, cabinet fixes, and shelving.
  • Light plumbing: fixture replacement, leak repairs, and drain clearing.
  • Flooring repairs: tile replacement, LVP repairs, carpet patches, and transitions.
  • Ceiling tile and grid: damaged tile replacement and grid repairs.
  • General property upkeep: anything your building needs to stay code-compliant and tenant-ready.

Who we work with

  • Commercial property management companies across the St. Louis metro.
  • Building owners with multi-tenant office, retail, or industrial portfolios.
  • Businesses handling tenant move-in and move-out turnovers.
