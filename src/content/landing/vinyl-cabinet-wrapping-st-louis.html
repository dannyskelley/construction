Fresh look. No replacement costs.

Replacing cabinetry or re-tiling an accent wall is expensive and disruptive. Abstract Construction LLC gives you a faster, more affordable path to a transformed space through professional vinyl wrapping and wallpaper installation with finishes that look custom and hold up to daily use.

Vinyl wrapping services

  • Cabinet wrapping: kitchen, bathroom, laundry, and commercial cabinetry wrapped in wood grain, solid color, or pattern vinyl.
  • Door and panel wrapping: interior doors, room dividers, and panel systems updated without replacement.
  • Commercial fixture wrapping: retail display cases, reception desks, and millwork refreshed with durable vinyl film.
  • Countertop edge wrapping: update laminate edges and exposed surfaces for a cohesive finished look.

Wallpaper installation services

  • Commercial wallpaper: Type II vinyl-coated wallcoverings for high-traffic offices, hotels, and restaurants.
  • Residential wallpaper: traditional, grasscloth, peel-and-stick, and specialty papers hung cleanly and level.
  • Accent walls: single-wall installations for boardrooms, lobbies, dining rooms, and bedrooms.
  • Wallpaper removal: clean, careful removal of existing wallcovering and surface prep before new installation.

Frequently asked questions

What surfaces can be wrapped with vinyl in St. Louis?

We wrap kitchen and bathroom cabinets, doors, countertop edges, furniture panels, commercial millwork, and retail fixtures. Vinyl wrapping works on most smooth or lightly textured surfaces.

Is vinyl wrapping better than painting cabinets?

Vinyl wrapping is a durable, moisture-resistant alternative to painting that holds up better in high-humidity areas like kitchens and bathrooms. It is also faster to install and can replicate wood grain or solid color finishes without the brush marks.

Do you hang commercial wallpaper in St. Louis?

Yes. We install commercial-grade and residential wallpaper, including vinyl-coated, fabric-backed, and peel-and-stick styles for offices, restaurants, hotels, retail stores, and homes across the St. Louis metro.
