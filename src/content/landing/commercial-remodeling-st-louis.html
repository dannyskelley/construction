St. Louis commercial remodeling that delivers on schedule

Whether you are refreshing a single suite or gut-renovating an entire floor, Abstract Construction LLC handles the full scope: demolition, framing, drywall, MEP coordination, flooring, ceilings, millwork, and final finishes. One crew, one point of contact, no surprises.

  • Office renovations: open-plan conversions, private office builds, conference rooms, and reception areas.

  • Medical and professional office remodels: exam rooms, waiting areas, and HIPAA-compliant layouts.
  • Common area upgrades: lobbies, restrooms, hallways, and breakrooms.

Frequently asked questions

How long does a commercial remodel take in St. Louis?

Most commercial remodels run 2 to 8 weeks depending on scope, permit timelines, and finish selections. We provide a milestone schedule before work begins.

Do you handle permits for commercial remodeling in Missouri?

Yes. We pull all required permits for St. Louis City, County, and Metro East jurisdictions and coordinate every inspection.

Can you remodel while my business stays open?

In most cases, yes. We phase work to minimize disruption and schedule noisy or dusty tasks during off hours when needed.
