Warehouse contractor with in-house structural steel

Abstract Construction LLC builds and expands warehouses, distribution space, and light manufacturing facilities across the St. Louis metro and Metro East Illinois. Because we fabricate and erect structural steel in-house, the most schedule-critical part of a warehouse project stays under our direct control — not a subcontractor's.

Warehouse construction services

  • Ground-up warehouse construction: pre-engineered metal buildings, conventional steel, and tilt-up.
  • Warehouse expansions: added bays, dock additions, and clear-height extensions tied cleanly into existing structures.
  • Mezzanines & equipment platforms: structural mezzanines fabricated and installed by our own crews.
  • Loading docks & drive-in doors: dock pits, levelers, seals, canopies, and oversized door openings.
  • Office build-outs inside shells: framed, finished, code-compliant office space within warehouse footprints.
  • Industrial fit-outs: demising walls, floor prep, lighting upgrades, and tenant-ready white-boxing.

Frequently asked questions

How much does warehouse construction cost in St. Louis?

Warehouse construction in the St. Louis metro typically runs $60–$140 per square foot for pre-engineered metal buildings and $120–$250 per square foot for conventional steel or tilt-up construction, depending on clear height, site work, and office build-out scope. We provide free line-item estimates after reviewing your site and requirements.

How long does it take to build a warehouse in St. Louis?

A typical 20,000–100,000 square foot warehouse takes 6–12 months from permits to occupancy. Pre-engineered metal buildings are usually faster than conventional construction. Expansions and interior fit-outs often finish in 2–4 months.

Do you build warehouse mezzanines and equipment platforms?

Yes. Abstract Construction fabricates and installs structural mezzanines, equipment platforms, stairs, and railings in-house, which keeps steel on schedule and eliminates a subcontractor markup.

Can you build out offices inside an existing warehouse?

Yes. We build code-compliant office space inside warehouse and manufacturing shells, including framing, drywall, electrical coordination, HVAC coordination, restrooms, and ADA-compliant finishes.
