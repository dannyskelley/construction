Streamlined design-build for St. Louis commercial projects

Traditional construction splits design and building into separate contracts, separate teams, and separate schedules. Abstract Construction LLC brings them together. From initial sketches through permit approval and final punch list, you have one point of contact and one accountable team.

What we design and build

  • Office buildings and build-outs: from ground-up structures to full interior fit-outs.
  • Retail and restaurant spaces: concept-ready layouts designed for operations and brand standards.
  • Warehouse and industrial facilities: efficient floor plans, loading access, and MEP coordination built in from the start.
  • Medical and professional offices: compliant layouts with finish packages that reflect your practice.

Design-build advantages

  • Single point of accountability for budget, schedule, and quality.
  • Early cost certainty because design decisions happen with real pricing data.
  • Faster permitting when the builder is involved in the drawing phase.
  • Fewer change orders because the team building the project helped design it.

Our design-build process

Step 1

Program and budget

We establish scope, square footage, function, and target budget before a single drawing is made.

Step 2

Design and permits

Our team produces construction drawings and manages the permit process through approval.

Step 3

Construction

We mobilize the crew, self-perform key trades, and coordinate subs for a single quality standard.

Step 4

Handover

Final walk-through, punch list completion, and certificate of occupancy delivered to you.

Frequently asked questions

What is design-build and how does it save money?

Design-build combines architecture, engineering, and construction under one contract. Because the team works together from day one, conflicts are caught early, schedules tighten, and you deal with one accountable party instead of three.

What types of projects do you design-build in St. Louis?

We handle office buildings, retail centers, restaurants, warehouses, and light industrial facilities throughout the St. Louis metro and Metro East Illinois.

How fast can a design-build project move?

Faster than traditional bid-build. Because design and construction overlap, many St. Louis projects break ground 4 to 8 weeks sooner than a conventional approach.
