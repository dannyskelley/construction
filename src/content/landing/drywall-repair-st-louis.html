Trusted St. Louis drywall contractor

Bad drywall work is impossible to hide. Every glance at the wall reminds you. We treat drywall as a finish craft, not a "good enough" trade. Whether you're fixing accident damage, prepping a unit for a new tenant, or finishing a remodel, our crew delivers walls that look untouched.

Drywall services we provide

  • Patches & hole repair: door knobs, accidents, picture hangers, electrical cut-ins.
  • Water damage drywall replacement: leak repairs, ceiling stains, full panel replacement.
  • Crack repair: settlement cracks at corners, doorways, and ceilings.
  • Texture matching: orange peel, knockdown, smooth, skip trowel.
  • Full hang & finish: additions, basements, garages, full remodels.
  • Commercial drywall: offices, retail, restaurants, tenant build-outs.

Frequently asked questions

How much does drywall repair cost in St. Louis?

Small patches (door knobs, nail pops) usually range from $150 to $400. Larger repairs from water damage or accidents typically run $400–$1,500 depending on square footage, texture matching, and paint. We provide free quotes on-site.

Can you match my existing wall texture?

Yes. We match orange peel, knockdown, smooth, skip trowel, and most popular St. Louis-area textures. After the patch dries we sand, prime, and re-texture so the repair blends invisibly.

Do you handle water-damaged drywall?

Yes. We remove damaged drywall and insulation, treat for mold when needed, and re-hang and finish to match the surrounding wall. We can coordinate directly with insurance adjusters.

How fast can you start?

Most small repairs are scheduled within 3–7 days. Emergency water damage and tenant move-out repairs are often started within 48 hours.
